Esther Aoko

Communication officer

Esther Aoko is a sexual and reproductive health youth advocate, feminist and community volunteer. She is a champion for women which she believes is her most important role in life. Esther is currently working as the communications officer at Positive Young Women Voices where she also facilitates mentorship sessions under the Adopt A girls month program.
