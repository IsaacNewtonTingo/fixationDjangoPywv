About us

Positive Young Women Voices (PYWV) is a community based organization which started as a self-help group in the year 2014 but later registered as a community based organization in the year 2017. The idea of group formation was derived when these advocates noticed there were major challenges facing young women and girls living with and at risk of HIV within the Nairobi slums of Dandora. The major challenges include teenage pregnancy, poverty, gender based violence, access to friendly health facility and information. Armed with experience they chose to fill the gaps by offering mentorship, provide linkages and referrals, create awareness and offer information. PYWV partners with other organisations nationally and internationally to achieve its mandate. They are NEPHAK, Dandora Hip Hop City, EGPAF, ICW, Sauti Skika, AYARHEP, PIPE among others.

Vision

A society where young women and girls in all their diversities attain their rights to health services, education as well as enjoy equal economic and social growth within their community. A sustainable humanization of women improving and strengthening the livelihoods of AGYW.

Mission

Empower and educate girls and young women through mentorship programs, workshops and seminars and disseminating information on sexual reproductive health, HIV, violence and discrimination. Promoting economic opportunities, assisting in education and employment possibilities and creating a safe environment to advance the life of young women.

Core values

We commit to these ourselves to the following core values and principles: Integrity and Accountability Innovation and creativity Heart for humanity Good Leadership and Equity Commitment and initiative

Activities

Empowerment

The empowerment program will be building the capacities of young women and girls affected and living with HIV to get the best care, treatment and support and to improve their lives with the right information as well as economic growth for sustainable livelihoods.

Prevention

PYWV prevention program will major on prevention with positive program where the young women who are influencers will act as mentors to the younger ones through psycho social support, peer education, facilitation of referrals for treatment ,screening and management of other non-communicable diseases and awareness creation.

Advocacy

We aim to advocate for universal access to quality health services, achievement of human rights and meaningful involvement of young women and adolescent girls in the center of policy creation and implementation. YWPV will also lobby to ensure that policy makers make and implement policies that are gender sensitive and non-discriminative for gender equality has not only been a challenge in Kenya but also in Africa ,the organization will spear head the advocacy to enhance this especially in Nairobi slums.

Networking and collaboration

For realization of positive outcomes of the programs networking and collaboration will be at the core of PYWV for we are not a service providing organization and hence the need to engage organizations and Institutions to see that members are effectively catered for .The organization will also engage other partners for the purpose of resources mobilization.

Care and Support

PYWV is intending to support the most vulnerable girls and young women in issues such as provision of sanitary towels, assisting them acquire bursaries for their school fees and the organization is intending to provide mentorship and sexuality education for girls and young women in the society .
