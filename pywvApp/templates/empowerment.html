Socio-economic empowerment




We have a socio_economic empowerment department to liberate adolescent girls and young women from the cycle of poverty by giving them resources to raise their community. This will also be attained by equipping them with financial and entrepreneurship training. It is structured to eradicate extreme poverty , link the women with job opportunities and also be able to provide start up kits for adolescent girls and young women living in Dandora
