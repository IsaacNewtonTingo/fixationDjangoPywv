Ending Violence Against Women and Girls (VAWG)

Behind every woman and girl there is an untold story and in most case is a sad story. Women and girls go through various challenges in life; gender based violence being the most rampant. This is due to the patriarchal system and cultural factors that deny women power to decide on their human rights. This triples amongst young women and girls living in informal settlements like Dandora. High rates of insecurity, lack of safe spaces, poverty, discrimination and lack of knowledge on their rights increases the vulnerability women and girls have to gender based violence. This also worsens as women not even aware if their rights are being violated. They end up living in constant fear. PYWV is able to provide a safe space where women and girls feel free to open up and share their life experiences. Through our monthly VAWG forums we have managed to empower as of January 2021, 100 young women on different forms of VAWG. The platform provides a safe space where young girls are equipped with skills to help them know how to go about in regards to their rights as well as identifying and responding to VAWG. Also we are able to create visibility on challenges experienced when seeking for support in case of incidence of VAWG. Our forums embrace the courage and resilience of young women and girls in life. Our main aim is to encourage women that they have the ability to be anything they envision and that behind their story there is victory, a drive to succeed and power to inspire others.
