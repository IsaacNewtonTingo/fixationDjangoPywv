Prevention and ending violence against women and girls




We are in a society where Women and Girls are not safe anywhere and if we are to stand in solidarity, we have to pledge to do something to free women and girls from the shackles of Violence. At Positive Young Women Voices , we are currently conducting a project, "Preventing and Ending Violence against women and Girls" .We engage young women in their diversity within the phases in Dandora including teenage mothers, survivors of Gender Based Violence and those most affected through trainingsâ€™ conducted bi-montly discussing root causes of Violence, principles of Gender and the impact it creates in women, girls and community at large from a range of perspectives.

In efforts of preventing and ending Violence against women and girls , we use a gender transformative intervention, involving young men and understanding the role they play as influencers and allies. We enhance victim/survivor centered response to Gender Based Violence by creating linkages with local authorities as well as referrals to the organizations and shelters responding to Gender Based Violence.

We trend the #ItEndsWithUs because we start a conversation, we believe the survivors, we empower and stand against violence against women and girls.
